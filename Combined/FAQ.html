Frequently Asked Questions

My pricing is $110 for a 50 minute session. That includes individual, family, marriage, couples or tele-therapy.

I do not accept insurance. It is possible to submit your paid invoices to your insurance to get reimbursed wholly or partially through your out of network benefits. Check with your insurance for details.

You may cancel up to 24 hours in advance with no penalty. Sessions canceled within 24 hours are subject to the full session fee.

  1. You’ll email, text, or call my wife Melissa (she makes all the big decisions) and she’ll get back with you within a couple hours with some possible appointment times.
  2. You’ll receive an email from me to access the client portal. Once you make an account, you’ll digitally sign some documents and fill out some paperwork telling me why you’re coming to therapy.
  3. Twenty-four hours before your appointment you’ll receive a text message from IVY Pay to put a card on file and pay for your upcoming session.
